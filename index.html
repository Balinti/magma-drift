About Magma Drift

Magma Drift is a free hyper-casual HTML5 game. Tap to hop between basalt pads on a volcanic ring. Avoid molten rocks, time near-misses for bonus points, and collect shields and coolant powerups. How long can you survive the rising heat?

How to play: Tap or press Space to hop to the next lane. Don't stay too long or the pad melts! Watch for telegraph rings warning of incoming strikes.
